# FirstCircle Product Specification

FirstCircle is a next-generation social connection platform designed to solve the problem of online-to-offline connection. Instead of swipe-matching individuals, FirstCircle matches users into small, curated groups of 3-6 people called **Circles** to attend real-world social activities called **Drops**.

---

## Core Concepts

### 1. Drops
A **Drop** is an activity proposal created by a user (the host) or generated by the system (Auto-Place).
*   **Activity Details**: Title, category (e.g., Board Games, Hiking, Foodie, Casual Drinks), description, location (coordinates and place name), and target event time.
*   **Capacity**: Mini-groups of 3 to 6 members (standard target is 4-5).
*   **Lifecycle**:
    1.  *Open*: Users browse and join the Drop, adding their "Vibe Votes".
    2.  *Matching*: The system matching engine runs when the drop closes or hits a matching threshold.
    3.  *Proposal*: A blind group proposal is generated.
    4.  *Confirmed*: All members accept, forming a Circle.
    5.  *Completed / Expired*: The event finishes, triggering reviews, or expires due to lack of participants.

### 2. Circles
A **Circle** is a confirmed group.
*   Once a proposal is accepted by all candidates, identities are revealed, a group chat room is unlocked, and details are locked.
*   Includes rescheduling controls for flexibility.

### 3. Vibe Votes
When joining a Drop, users specify their preferred vibe (e.g., "Deep Conversation", "Energetic & Active", "Chill & Relaxed"). The engine groups people with similar vibe preferences.

### 4. Reliability Score
Users start with a baseline reliability score of 100.
*   **On-Time Arrival**: +2 points.
*   **Late Arrival**: -5 points.
*   **No-Show (Late Cancel <24h)**: -15 points.
*   **No-Show (Without warning)**: -30 points.
*   Low reliability scores (e.g., <75) restrict matching priority.

### 5. Soft Blacklist
If a user skips another user's proposal or reports them for bad behavior, they are placed on a soft blacklist. The matching engine avoids placing these users in the same Circle in the future.
